Our Mission

General capabilities within the our Curriculum make up a set of knowledge, skills, behaviors and dispositions that can be developed through all the learning areas: Literacy, Numeracy, Information and communication technology competence, Critical and creative thinking

We have a School Nurse or Health Clerk on campus 5 days/week. Your child will be evaluated and treated immediately. You will be called right away if the injury is severe, or if your child needs to go home due to illness.

We highly recommend you to let your child learn one foreign language because it's very easy to learn another language in their childhood. But language course is optional.

All children will be walked to the pick up lines under the shade structure on the east side of the parking lot. Children will be supervised at all times. You may either wait in the car line for driveway pick up or park your car in on the street or in the St. James lot and walk to pick up your child. Please wait outside for your child.

You can purchase uniforms at the PTA uniform sale before the first day of school, or at any major retailer, including Target, Sears, JCPenney, WalMart, The Children’s Place and Land’s End.
