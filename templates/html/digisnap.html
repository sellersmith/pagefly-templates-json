Remarkable Low-Light Performance

Capture stunning images in challenging lighting conditions with the large f/1.2 aperture. Ideal for portrait photography, weddings, and events, the ProOptix VisionMaster 85mm lens delivers exceptional low-light performance.

Superior Optics for Unmatched Image Quality

Featuring 10 elements in 8 groups, including two aspherical and one low-dispersion element, this lens ensures edge-to-edge sharpness and minimized chromatic aberration. The nine-blade rounded diaphragm produces a smooth bokeh.

Smooth, Silent Autofocus & Image Stabilization

Equipped with an advanced ultrasonic autofocus motor, the VisionMaster lens offers fast, accurate, and quiet focusing. The built-in 4-stop image stabilization system allows you to capture sharp, blur-free images even when shooting handheld.
