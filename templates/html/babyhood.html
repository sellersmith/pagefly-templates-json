How To Wash Your Newborn Clothes

You can wash baby clothes with the rest of your laundry, but avoid harsh detergents and fabric softeners. Detergents labeled "delicate" or "gentle" are less irritating to the baby's skin. Clothes with poop on them need to be soaked in nappy sanitiser before washing.

What Kinds Of Clothes Do Newborns Need?

Clothing should be comfortable, soft and easy to care for. A jumpsuit with elastic front and top ties with a turn-up collar that is easy to pull over the baby's head is best. Jumpsuits with zippers make changing baby quick and easy.
