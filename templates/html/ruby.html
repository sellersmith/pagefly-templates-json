How to protect your socks

Learn some simple tips for sock care

Wash socks in cold water

Hot water can cause your socks to shrink and lose their shape. To keep your socks looking their best, we recommend to wash them in cold water.

Avoid fabric softeners

Fabric softeners can leave a residue, affecting their absorbency and breathability. Opt for a more natural solution like adding a little vinegar.

Hang dry your socks

Avoid putting your socks in the dryer, as the high heat can cause them to shrink. Instead, you can hang your socks to dry on a clothesline or drying rack.

Store socks properly

To keep your socks in good condition, we recommend to store them in a dry, cool place. Avoid storing them in direct sunlight or damp areas
