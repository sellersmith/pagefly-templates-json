How To Use Essential Oils

Essential oils can be used in many different ways, depending on the specific oil and the desired outcome. Some of the most popular methods include:

Aromatherapy

Simply add a few drops to your diffuser to enjoy the therapeutic benefits of essential oils.

Topical application

Apply a few drops directly to your skin to help soothe, heal, and improve the appearance of your skin.

Inhalation

Inhale the scent of the oil directly from the bottle or use it in a diffuser for a more intense experience.
